Decentralized Dwellings: Homestay On The Blockchain.

I provide solid and unique product design services such as readymade websites, mobile applications and website development for tech companies

Project’s goal

This app offers a host of fresh experiences, unlike traditional homestay booking applications. Users will primarily interact with an AI virtual assistant within the app. Based on provided criteria, the system automatically suggests suitable apartments. This may initially feel unconventional, potentially causing some hesitation as this approach is not yet widely adopted, and users may not be accustomed to it. However, once familiar, this experience is refreshingly inventive and even enjoyable, aligning with the current trend of engaging with AI virtual assistants.

The challenge

My mission is to create groundbreaking, intuitive experiences by blending advanced technology with user-friendly design. This app embodies the limitless potential of human-computer interaction.

Problem discovery

Context

Blockchain adoption in travel faces hurdles like usability and trust, with users often overwhelmed by technical jargon and poor interfaces. Travelers need a single, intuitive platform combining NFT asset management and secure bookings.

The “WHAT”

Innovative Solution

A blockchain-integrated wallet functioning as a digital passport for secure travel planning and bookings.

Key features

- NFT-based booking verification
- AI-powered travel recommendations
- Secure smart-contract transactions
